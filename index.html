About Us

WHO WE ARE

We are an interdisciplinary community of undergraduates, postgraduates and early career researchers (ECRs) dedicated to achieving positive impact through medical technology and biomedical innovation.

Based at Monash University, founded in late 2018. Open to all, regardless of home institution, discipline or experience.

WHAT WE DO

We're here to help break down silos within the Medical Technology sector in Melbourne.

Front and center, MEDHACK is our thing. It's a 2.5 day hackathon for the doers, tinkerers, dreamers of Melbourne to descend and cook up potentially the next big thing in MedTech. This year's theme is assistive technology.
